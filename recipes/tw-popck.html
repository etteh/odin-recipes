Taichung Popcorn Chicken

Popcorn chicken's name is funny. It's not exactly popcorn. Popcorn is the result of heated corn kernels. Is popcorn chicken a result of heated chicken? It kind of is.

But you should not expect it to be ready made within two minutes in a microwave. Here's a simple recipe that explains step-by-step how to make popcorn chicken just like the kind found in Taichung's Fenchia Night Market.

Ingredients

    For the Chicken and Marinade:

  • 1.5 pounds of chicken breasts or thighs
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of shaoxing wine or orange juice
  • 1/2 tsp of sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of 5-spice powder
  • 1/2 tsp of white pepper
  • 1/4 tsp of salt

    • For the Batter:

  • 1/4 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 2 tbsps of sweet potato starch
  • 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • 1/4 tsp of white pepper

    • Finishing add:

  • a handful of Thai basil leaves
